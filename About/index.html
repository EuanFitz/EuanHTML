My name is Euan Fitzpatrick. I'm a 23 year old student at SAIT. I moved to Whistler when I was 19 without knowing anything about the town or anyone in it. I made some really cool friends who came from all around the world, Australia, England, Scotland, Germany, New Zealand. I developed a deep love for skiing as whistler was a hub for all things snow. I spent the last 2 summers planting trees which has been an amazing experience and probably the hardest thing I've ever done or ever will do.

My Hobbies include skateboarding, hiking, skiing/snowboarding, biking, camping, pretty much all things outdoors.
